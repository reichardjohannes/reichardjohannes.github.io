Work / Sybit · Internal / White-Label Portals
Case Study · 2024 – 2025

White-Label Portals — from concept to customer in days.

Configurable portal templates on SAP BTP that let the sales team show prospects a fully branded, interactive demo, not a slide deck. Two live examples built to lower the first barrier and turn interest into project commitment.

Client
Sybit · Internal
Role
UI/UX Design Lead
Year
2024 – 2025
Platform
SAP BTP
Focus
Sales Enablement
Status
Live · Editable
Challenge

Slides don't sell portals

Prospects needed to see and feel what their future customer portal could look like, but building individual demos for each pitch was too slow and too expensive.

My role

Design, build, enable

Responsible for UI/UX design, visual direction and template architecture. Created reusable portal templates that the sales team can rebrand and present live without design or dev support.

Outcome

Show, don't tell

Two fully functional white-label portals live on SAP BTP. Sales uses them in every pitch prospects see their brand on a working product within minutes.

Example 01 · Iqony

Energy customer portal. Desktop & mobile.

A white-label portal designed for energy providers. Dashboard, meter readings, invoices, consumption charts everything a residential customer needs, branded to Iqony within hours. Built responsive from the start.
