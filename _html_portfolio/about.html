/02 · Bio

From agency floor to senior consultant.

My focus sits in eCommerce, SAP Commerce, customer portals, fleet and machine management platforms. I work best in interdisciplinary teams alongside internal and external developers, where strong communication is the difference between a good design and one that actually ships.

I own the whole UX path: client communication, research, workshops, wireframes, testing, alignment, and delivery. I speak the language of PO/PL stakeholders just as well as the dev team on the other side of the handover.

AI is changing how we design, and I lean into that. I use it across the entire workflow, from research synthesis to prototyping to delivery. Not as a shortcut, but as a tool that makes every phase faster and sharper. Better iterations, better insights, better outcomes for the team and the end user.

/03 · Process

Phase 0 → Delivery.

Six steps. Every project, the same spine. Adapted to context, never skipped.

00
Phase 0: Alignment
Kickoff workshops with PO & PL. Stakeholder map, success metrics, working agreement. Before any design work begins.
Stakeholder map · Metrics
01
Research & Audit
Heuristic evaluation, user interviews across primary personas, pain-point maps and journey maps to surface where the real breaks are.
Personas · Journey maps
02
Wireframes & IA
Lo-fi wireframes in Balsamiq, three review rounds. Sitemaps that collapse legacy complexity before a pixel is drawn.
Sitemap · WF v1–v3
03
Testing
Moderated usability tests with 6–10 real users on the v3 wireframes. No design ships without test evidence and measurable deltas.
Protocols · Findings
04
Hi-Fi & Component Library
Final design in Sketch or Figma. Component library aligned with platform conventions, co-authored with dev leads.
Hi-Fi · Spec doc
05
Scrum Delivery
Embedded UX in 2-week sprints. Every standup, refinement, review. Continuous QA through acceptance. Zero design-vs-build disputes.
Live since launch
